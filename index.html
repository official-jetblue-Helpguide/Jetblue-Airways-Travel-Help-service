  6. Consider using the JetBlue mobile app for account-specific help.
  7. Request a callback if the option is offered while on hold.
  8. Use email for non-urgent issues and provide complete details.
  9. Message JetBlue on Twitter or Facebook for additional support.
  10. Visit the airport ticket counter for in-person help if you are traveling soon.

Best Times to Call

To minimize wait times, avoid calling on weekends, holidays, or during peak travel hours. Early morning and late-night calls usually have the shortest wait.
